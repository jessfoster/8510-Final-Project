November 12, 1800 

Admitted Jonas Kollman a Seaman, was born in Germany, is twenty-two years of age was taken by the French and carried into Guadeloupe. He came to this port four and an half years ago, and has sailed out of it ever since in different Merchants employ the last of which was in the Ship [?], Captain George, belonging to Daniel W. Cox of this City, Merchant, on board of which Vessel he had the misfortune of breaking one of his thigh bones on board said ship; as they weighing the Anchor, the Pall of the Windless not catching, the hand spike struck his thigh and broke it. This accident happened thirteen months ago, and it has never been set, there being no Doctor on board the Vessel.
