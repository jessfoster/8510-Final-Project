November 28, 1800 

Admitted George Crouse he don't appear to have gained any legal residence, is a German and landed in New York and continued in that City three years, constantly in the capacity of an Hostler, and for seven or eight years since has been backwards and forwards from that City to this, likewise to and from Charleston, South Carolina as a Gentleman Waitingman, is now been about three weeks in this City, is sick and much swelled in his Body & legs twenty seven years of age.
