February 12, 1803 

Admitted Catharine Stewart Legal Resident 20 years of age a Native of Philadelphia; says she lived as an house-maid with Jon Stonemetz in Arch street three weeks, and that he gave her the Venereal disease. She then went and lived with Peggy Smith (a house of bad fame in Cherry Alley) several weeks, and is now come here to be cured of said disease.
