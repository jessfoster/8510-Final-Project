April 11, 1803 

Admitted Esther Vanse Legal Resident 28 years of age, born in Ireland, and landed in this City twelve years ago, and went to Cranberry County of Middlesex, New Jersey, and lived three years, then returned to Philadelphia and has lived in it ever since. Her Husband Matthew Vanse is now in Jail for beating and abusing her, the effects of which has rendered her incapable of working.
