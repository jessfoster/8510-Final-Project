January 16, 1801

Admitted Patrick M. Gehen has legal residence, he was born in Colewain, Ireland, and came to this City in the Ship Happy Return, Captain McCalley, five Years ago, and lived and followed the business of a Plasterer in it ever since. He is Sick and much swelled in his face, has had the Venereal disease, and after having taken of Mercurial pills, he caught cold, which is the cause of his present and sad indisposition, and is now rendered unable to work for a livelihood.
