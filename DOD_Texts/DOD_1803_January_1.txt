January 1, 1803 

Admitted Rebecah Smith Legal Resident 20 years of age, born in New Jersey, came with her parents to Philadelphia when a Child, and continued in it ever since, and these two years past she has lived with Mr. Kitchen (City Tavern and Merchants Coffeehouse) in the Character of a Chambermaid, and about three weeks ago she fell down and hurt one of her knees, so badly that She has not been able to walk since, and is sent here.
