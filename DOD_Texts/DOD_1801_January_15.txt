January 15, 1801 

Admitted Matthew Morin and his Wife Sarah Morin, Old Customers. They were both Discharged the 11th March 1800. He is a Gardener, and goes out in the Spring to follow Gardening all Summer & autumn, then comes here to Garden and keep himself warm all winter and get Cloathed, then take a trip in the Spring again. They have some household furniture which is pretty good, and is now in the Hall, and to mend the matter she is Pregnant.
