February 21, 1803 

Admitted John Quinn 24 years old, born in Ireland, landed at New-Castle, Delaware State three years ago; then went to Lancaster State of Pennsylvania, and lived three months, after that he worked through the Country with different Farmers, but not long enough in one place to have gained a legal residence. Says he has lived in Philadelphia these eleven months past, and followed labouring for a livelihood, and is now come here, afflicted with pains in his limbs, and the Venereal disease.
