November 17, 1800 

Admitted Catharine Groves and her Children, Nancy Groves One year and seven months Old, John Groves five weeks Old her husband, John Groves, is in Jail and she says is sentenced to fourteen years imprisonment for killing a Horse and skinning it. Having these young children and no friends to assist her, she was advised to make application to the Guardians of the Poor, which she did and is come here.
