October 4, 1800

Admitted Mary Hyland and her Child Rosannah fifteen Months old, she's also pregnant with another. Her Husband James Hyland is gone to Sea in the Ship George Washington, and left a Power of Attorney with Jacob Edwards, Taylor, in Water Street between Market and Chestnut streets, to receive for her use, of the Owner of said ship, seven dollars monthly, and said power, she says, is put into the hands of A. Musgrave who sent her here by a Watchman accompanied by his Order of Admission.
