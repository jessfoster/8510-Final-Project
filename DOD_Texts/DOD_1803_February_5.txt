February 5, 1803 

Admitted Ann Gibbs Legal Resident 39 years of age, and her three Children Margaret Dunn 6 years, Ann Dunn years & Thomas Gibbs (by her present Husband Jonathan Gibbs) near 2 years Old. She came from near little York, ten years ago, and has lived in Philadelphia ever since. Her Husband is an Oisterman, but is very poor and unable to support his Wife and Children, and they are come here.
