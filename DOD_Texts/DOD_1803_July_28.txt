July 28, 1803

Admitted Mary Moyers Legal Resident 12 years of age, born in Philadelphia. Her parents are dead and she was bound to Jacob Plume in Plumb Street between Second and Third street, Southwark, Baker, and on account of his not giving her sufficient cloathing and food, she left him two weeks ago last Saturday and went to Mrs. Matsons in Race Street between Front and Second street and staid two weeks; and since then she has been with Mrs. Maria Course in 5th between Race and Vine street and is now sent here.
