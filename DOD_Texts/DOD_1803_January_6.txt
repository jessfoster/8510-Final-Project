January 6, 1803

Admitted Mary Perkins Legal Resident 26 years of age, and her son Matthew Perkins aged 5 years. She was brought to bed six months ago with a still-born child, but previous to which her base husband, Samuel Powell Perkins gave her the Venereal disease, and of which she is at present affected; and also in a kind of Melancholy stupor occassioned no doubt by the brutish and cruel conduct of her unnatural Husband and father of her three children, who has now absconded and abandoned his family to the World's care.
