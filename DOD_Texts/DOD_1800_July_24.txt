July 24, 1800

Died Ann Barber after a long and tedious consumptive indisposition expired this afternoon. Admitted Feb. 15th 1797. Credit Norther Liberties. 

Discharged Jacob Felsy, who was admitted the 23rd ultimo with the Venereal disease and is now gone out and George Latimer Esquire is responsible for medical assistance and nursing him from the time of his admission to this data? is 4 weeks & a half at $3 per week ... $13.50.
