January 22, 1803 

Admitted Margaret Harris Legal Resident 24 years of age a Native of Germantown, served her time with Frederick Colly of same place; has lived in Philadelphia these three years past and rented a Room during that time. She married Robert Harris, a Sailor, who has been gone to Sea these seven months past, and has not been heard of since. She is now afflicted with the Venereal disease, which situation renders her incapable of working for Support, and is come in.
