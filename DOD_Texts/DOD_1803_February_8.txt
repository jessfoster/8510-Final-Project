February 8, 1803

Admitted Anne Boyle Legal Resident 20 years of age, and her two Children Edward Boyle 3 years old the 9th of last September and Ann Boyle 11 months old the 25th Ultimo; Patrick Boyle, the Husband and Father of her Children, lives in Catharine Street Number 124, Southwark and by profession is a Stevidore or a Stower of Vessels Cargoes. But from excess of drink and abuse of his Wife, and neglect of their Children, obliged her to come here.
