July 6, 1800

Eloped James Loddo the little english Cockney, who was admitted the 30th last January very sick, and a bad swelling in his Groin, and much distressed. This makes three times that he has scaled the fence, being the most ready and easiest way of getting his liberty. Credit Northern Liberties. 

Died Catherine Beale, widow, an Aged infirm woman who was admitted the 20th March last, far advanced in a decay, & this day expired at 5 o'clock PM. Credit City.
