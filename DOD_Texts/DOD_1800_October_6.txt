October 6, 1800 

Admitted Mary Berry a Mulatto young Woman twenty three years of age, of legal residence, is far advanced in her pregnancy & says the Father of the Child that she now bears is Named Matthias Clay, a Member of Congress and to whom she has swore it before Gunning Bedford, Esquire. Said Mary lived with the Widow Sadie in Arch street (and at the same time Clay boarded there) between seventh & eighth streets.
