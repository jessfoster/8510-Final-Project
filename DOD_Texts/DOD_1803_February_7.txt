February 7, 1803 

Admitted Mary Smith Legal Resident 54 years of age, came with her Parents from Prussia when a Child. She's the Wife of William Smith, Brewers Alley, Cordwainer, who has deserted her these three years past and took up with another woman, but where he is, she cannot tell; they have been married upwards of six and twenty years, and have had several children, the oldest, a Son, is 25, but worthless, and neglects taking care of himself; and from sickness, poverty, & distress, she has come here.
